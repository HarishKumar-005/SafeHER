# SafeHer AR — Devpost Submission Data

## 🚀 Pitch (The "Elevator Pitch")
SafeHer AR is an Augmented Reality women's safety platform that projects real-time, community-verified safety hotspots and routes directly onto the real world via your smartphone camera, keeping eyes up and users aware.

## ⚠️ The Problem
Navigating cities safely, especially at night or in unfamiliar areas, is an ongoing challenge for women. Traditional 2D map applications demand constant downward attention, fracturing situational awareness when users need it most. Furthermore, existing map tools lack the hyper-local, community-sourced safety data—like "broken streetlights," "isolated walkways," or "known harassment zones"—that truly informs safe transit. 

## 💡 The Solution (Our Innovation)
SafeHer AR bridges the digital-physical divide by projecting dynamic, location-based safety warnings seamlessly into augmented reality. 

Instead of staring at a map, users hold their phone up to see exactly *where* a risk is located along their path. SafeHer AR combines anonymous community reporting with a sensor-driven AR engine. High-risk zones auto-trigger Text-to-Speech (TTS) and haptic alerts, allowing users to keep their phone down while maintaining complete awareness of their surroundings. 

## 🌟 Why This Will Win
- **Innovative use of tech:** Repurposes standard smartphone sensors (CameraX, magnetometer, GPS) to build a fast, lightweight AR experience without the overhead of heavy AR engines.
- **Critical social impact:** Addresses a universal, pressing problem (women's safety) with a pragmatic, user-centric solution.
- **High-polish execution:** Features an accessible, soft-feminine UI, collision-aware label rendering, TTS for eyes-free safety, and a fully realized community impact dashboard.

## 🎥 Judges Demo Guide (What to Try)
1. **Experience the AR View:** Tap the AR tab and slowly spin the device. Notice how the safety labels float seamlessly and stack automatically without overlapping thanks to collision algorithms.
2. **Open a Detail Sheet:** Tap the `"⋯"` on any floating `ARInlineLabel`. Observe the smooth bottom-sheet animation revealing a 300-word safety context and instant SOS/Navigation buttons.
3. **Listen to the Auto-TTS:** If a "HIGH" or "URGENT" risk anchor loads within 200 meters, feel the haptic pulse and listen as the app proactively reads the hazard via Text-to-Speech without any screen interaction required.
4. **Submit a Fast Report:** Hit the main "+" fab to experience the frictionless, anonymous 3-step reporting flow used to crowd-source safety data.
